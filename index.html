Determined to provide the best customer experience I quickly found my list of clients growing quickly presenting the challenge of keeping up with an increase in requests. Without the ability to add more hours in a day to address it all I found myself evaluating processes, innovating, and creating better methods to increase productivity. Excel became my new best friend as I developed templates to compose quotes, invoices, and reports quickly with ease. Soon discovering as productivity increased so did the number of requests I would receive forcing me to reevaluate and continue to innovate.
